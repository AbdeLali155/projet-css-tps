The idea that safety = surveillance
The Evidence
When people know they are being watched, they stop speaking freely. This is the Chilling Effect.
Giving up privacy is not a trade-off — it is a false choice. Surveillance systems fail, get hacked, and are misused. Safety is not guaranteed.
Self-Censorship — students stop speaking freely
Reduced Assembly — protests become psychologically inhibited
False Positives — innocent students wrongfully flagged
Data Breaches — biometric databases are prime targets
The Design
Anonymized heat-mapping — no faces, no names
Detect patterns, not people. No faces. No names. No stored data. Footage is processed and discarded in real-time.
